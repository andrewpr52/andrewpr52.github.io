About Psst

Psst is a new, exciting, messaging service like nothing you've seen before!

Psst is a micro-blogging website created as an assignment for COMP249 at Macquarie University.

You must be logged in to make a post. Messages up to 150 characters can be posted using Psst. All Pssts are public and can be viewed at the Psst Home page.
